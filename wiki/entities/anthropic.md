# Anthropic

Frontier AI lab, maker of [[entities/claude-code]]
[2026-06-24-2025-llm-year-in-review].

## In these sources

[[entities/andrej-karpathy]] credits Anthropic with getting an important "order of
precedence" right: rather than running agents in cloud containers (as he argues
[[entities/openai]] did first), Anthropic prioritized **the already-existing, booted-up
local computer** — its installation, context, data, secrets, configuration, and
low-latency interaction — and packaged Claude Code into "a delightful, minimal CLI form
factor." The result reframed what AI looks like: "not just a website you go to like
Google… a little spirit/ghost that lives on your computer," a new interaction paradigm
[2026-06-24-2025-llm-year-in-review]. See [[concepts/ghosts-vs-animals]].
