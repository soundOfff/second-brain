# Google Gemini (Nano Banana)

Google's multimodal LLM family. Its **"Nano Banana"** image model is singled out by
[[entities/andrej-karpathy]] as "one of the most incredible, paradigm-shifting models of
2025" [2026-06-24-2025-llm-year-in-review].

## In these sources

- Nano Banana is framed as a first hint of the [[concepts/llm-gui]] — its power comes not
  from image generation alone but from the **joint capability of text generation, image
  generation, and world knowledge** tangled in one model's weights
  [2026-06-24-2025-llm-year-in-review].
- In the [[concepts/software-3-0]] version of MenuGen, you give a menu photo to Gemini
  and ask it to "use Nano Banana to overlay" dish images directly onto the menu pixels —
  collapsing most of the old app stack [2026-06-24-sequoia-ascent-2026-summary].

> Stub — appears only via the Nano Banana image-generation capability.
