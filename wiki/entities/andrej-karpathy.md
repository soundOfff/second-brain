# Andrej Karpathy

AI researcher, engineer, and educator. He co-founded [[entities/openai]], led Autopilot
at Tesla, and founded **Eureka Labs** (an AI-instructor education company)
[2026-06-24-sequoia-ascent-2026-summary]. He is a prolific source author in this brain
(captured via the `andrej-karpathy` feed) and the originator of several framings the
wiki is organized around.

## Originator of the LLM Wiki method

Credited with the **[[concepts/llm-wiki]]** method for building a
[[concepts/second-brain]] — letting an LLM own and maintain the knowledge base. His own
instance reportedly grew to ~100 articles / ~400,000 words, maintained by the agent while
he worked normally [2026-06-23-karpathy-llm-wiki]. He returns to LLM knowledge bases as
his clearest example of work that was "impossible before but is now natural," and as a
tool for [[concepts/outsourcing-thinking-vs-understanding|understanding]]
[2026-06-24-sequoia-ascent-2026-summary].
